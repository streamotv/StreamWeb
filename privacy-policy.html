Streamo TV

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2024

1. Information We Collect

Streamo TV is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect or store:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Account credentials (the app doesn't require registration)
  • Payment information (the app is completely free)

2. Analytics and Crash Reporting

We use anonymous analytics to:

  • Monitor app performance and stability
  • Track basic usage patterns (without identifying users)
  • Fix crashes and improve user experience

This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

3. Advertising

Streamo TV uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Non-personal identifiers for ad targeting
  • Approximate location (country-level, not precise)

Premium Features & Bright Data Network

In return for some premium features of StreamoTV (like removing ads), you may choose to be a peer on the Bright Data network. By enabling this option:

  • You agree to have read and accepted the Bright SDK EULA
  • You acknowledge the Bright Data Privacy Policy
  • You may opt out at any time by disabling "Web Indexing" in the app's settings menu

This optional program helps support the development of StreamoTV while providing you with premium features.

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

4. Watch History

The app temporarily stores:

  • Recently watched videos (only on your device)
  • Playback progress for your convenience

This data is not uploaded to our servers and is deleted when you clear the app cache.

5. Permissions

Streamo TV requests these Android permissions:

  • INTERNET: To stream video content
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To check connection quality

6. Children's Privacy

Streamo TV is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Policy Changes

We will notify users of any privacy policy changes:

  • Through in-app notifications
  • By updating the "Last Updated" date on this page
